

Day 1: Arrival in Goa
- Depart from Delhi via an evening flight (approx. 4 hours).
- Upon arrival at Goa International Airport, a private transfer will be arranged for a smooth check-in at The Leela Goa, a 5-star luxury resort.
- Check-in and relax at the resort.
- Enjoy a welcome dinner at the resort's signature restaurant, showcasing Goan and international cuisine.

Estimated cost: INR ₹ 25,000 - 30,000 (Airfare, private transfer, accommodation, and welcome dinner)

Day 2: Explore North Goa
- Begin the day with a visit to the famous Calangute Beach for water sports and relaxation.
- Head to the nearby Fort Aguada for a historical tour and stunning views of the Arabian Sea.
- Enjoy a luxurious lunch at the resort.
- In the evening, explore the bustling Calangute Market for shopping and local delicacies.

Estimated cost: INR ₹ 10,000 - 15,000 (Entrance fees, water sports, lunch, and shopping)

Day 3: Wildlife and Adventure
- Spend the day at the Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary and Dudhsagar Waterfalls, engaging in a guided tour and jeep safari.
- Return to the resort for a rejuvenating spa treatment.
- Enjoy a special dinner prepared by the resort's master chef, featuring Goan and international cuisine.

Estimated cost: INR ₹ 20,000 - 25,000 (Tour, spa treatment, and special dinner)
